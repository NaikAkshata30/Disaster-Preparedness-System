🎯 What is Disaster Preparedness?

Disaster preparedness refers to the measures taken to prepare for and reduce the effects of disasters. It involves planning, organizing resources, training, and preparing communities to respond effectively when disaster strikes. Being prepared can save lives, reduce economic losses, and help communities recover faster.

💡 Did you know? Studies show that communities with high disaster preparedness reduce casualties by up to 60% and economic losses by 40%.
